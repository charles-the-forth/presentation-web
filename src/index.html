CanSat

We are team of students participating in CanSat competition. Our goal is to make a satellite size of can. We're not alone, we also have two androids in our team - EVA and TOM.

EVA

EVA (Environmental Vocation Architecture) is our satellite and core part of our mission. She will measure many attributes during her fall.
Measured values:

  • Temperature

  • Atmospheric pressure

  • Altitude

  • Rotation

  • Speed

  • GPS

TOM

TOM (Technical Operation Machine) is our base station. He is connected to antenna and receives data which are sent by EVA. These data are display on TOM's display and also are sent via USB to connected computer.
